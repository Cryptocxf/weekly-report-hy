## Description: <br>
Weekly Report Hy formats user-provided Chinese work notes into a structured, professionally worded weekly report without adding unsupported facts. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[cryptocxf](https://clawhub.ai/user/cryptocxf) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees and teams use this skill to turn brief Chinese weekly work notes into categorized, polished weekly reports for status updates or internal reporting. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: User notes may contain client names, internal project details, amounts, or other confidential information. <br>
Mitigation: Review pasted notes before use and ask the agent to redact or generalize sensitive details when the report may be shared. <br>
Risk: Professional rewriting can make vague source notes sound more certain than the underlying facts support. <br>
Mitigation: Compare the generated report against the original notes and remove any detail that was not supplied or reasonably grounded. <br>
